Kale Salad Recipe

A perfect accompaniment to almost any main meal

Ingredients

  • 2 Bunches dark kale
  • 2 brown pears
  • 200g Gran Padano cheese
  • 50mL Honey
  • 100g Walnuts, halved

Steps

  1. Cut Kale into a fine chiffonade
  2. Using a peeler, create thin strands and sheets of Gran Padano
  3. Finely slice the pears
  4. drizzle honey over walnuts in a small bowl
  5. Combine all ingredients into a serving bowl
  6. Dress with salt, pepper, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar to taste

And voila! Enjoy your meal!
