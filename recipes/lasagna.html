Lasagna is a classic italian supper. Perfect as a hot dish to get you through those cold winter nights.

Ingredients

White Sauce
  • 1L Milk
  • 200g Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 400g flour
The rest
  • 6 x lasagna pasta sheets
  • 1kg lean beef mince
  • 800g tinned tomatoes
  • 300g mozzarella
  • 2 medium-sized brown onions
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • zucchini, carrot, celery
  • Bay leaf
  • 100mL pinot noir

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180ËšC
  2. Make the pasta sauce
  3. Make the white sauce
  4. Build the lasagna by cycling layers of pasta sacue, white sauce, and pasta sheets
  5. Put it in the oven
  6. Take it out the oven

And voila! Enjoy your meal!
